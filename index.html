FluxGlove
Lore of the FluxGlove
Inspired by the gauntlets of ancient smiths, this glove translates the oldest human tool — the hand — into the language of machines. Each flex sensor was hand-calibrated over days of iteration, every µV of drift corrected in firmware written at 2am.

A hand-worn VR interface: custom-fabricated flex sensors map finger curvature in real-time while IMU fusion tracks absolute wrist orientation — translating the full complexity of human hand motion into clean digital signals. Built from scratch at both PCB and firmware level, with a software layer for gesture recognition and calibration drift correction.

Lore of VerifyMed
Where Rivendell's council deliberated for days, this system validates in seconds. An army of AI agents that never sleeps, never tires, never misses a discrepancy buried in a thousand-row compliance table.

An agentic AI pipeline solving healthcare's credential-verification bottleneck. The system orchestrates multiple AI agents to cross-reference, validate, and flag discrepancies across fragmented provider databases — automating a process that previously required entire compliance teams working across weeks.

Lore of VenueSync
MAASK — the fellowship forged not in fire, but in deadline pressure. Five engineers, one agentic engine, zero seconds to spare. The 800ms bifurcation threshold was born on a 3am whiteboard.

Co-built VenueSync — a dual-input emergency detection system for hospitality venues. Combines AI surveillance over existing CCTV with a guest-initiated PWA via disposable check-in credentials.
